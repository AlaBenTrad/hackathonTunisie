Arab Hack

Within the framework of the Arab regional event on the digital cultural economy, the Arab Organization for Education, Culture and Science, in partnership with the Tunis International Center for Digital Cultural Economy, is organizing the Arab Hackathon (ArabHack), in which young people from different Arab countries gather to send digital cultural projects for 48 consecutive hours. It is applicable in at least two Arab countries This is to link the relations between Arab youth in cultural creativity to develop joint Arab digital projects that value Arab culture through modern technologies.

If you are students, freelancers, teachers, tourism professionals, startups, leaders you take part at this hackathon.

Participants in the hackathon work on imagination and creativity to create a prototype and provide innovative solutions or services for information technology to value heritage and cultural contents in the Arab world. Participants must work in teams not exceeding 3 people from more than one Arab country (in case this condition is not met for the group, this will be done by the supervisors to form the group from more than one Arab country) The proposed projects concern more than one Arab country or an Arab regional field under the supervision of specialists and professors in order to create a framed and systematic collective intelligence through which their projects will be presented at the end of the competition before a qualified expert committee to determine the winning team after this challenge.
