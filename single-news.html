PaxHack Fintech & Blockchain Hackathon

PAX HACK - THE BLOCKCHAIN FEST HACKATHON powered by Paxful!

Join us for an exciting blockchain development hackathon. Applications for Initial Concepts and Ideas on Blockchain will start with the Kick-off event on Saturday, 27 November, 2021! A 24-hour event that will be open to students and individuals in the professional community. The hackathon is powered by Paxful and Vsober, and organised in cooperation with IEEE SMU (South Mediterranean University), MSC SMU and GDSC SMU.

Paxful, a leading global peer-to-peer fintech platform is here to bring developers and technology enthusiasts together to contribute to the future of blockchain technologies and transform the technological landscape.

The Kick-off event will feature keynote speaker Shantnoo Saxsena - MENA Advisor to Paxful, Youssef Belguith - Hackathon Mentor, technical workshops and use-case presentations. Are you ready to prove your creativity and tech knowledge to everyone with your groundbreaking ideas ? Then this competition is for your team and you.
