About Hackathon Tunisia

Hackathon Tunisia , online hackathon, makeathon, game jam, ideathon, datathon, designathon etc. in our country and in the world. organizing and announcing events; It is a voluntary community that aims to teach and spread
the hackathon culture to everyone.


HACKATHON ORGANIZING

Hackathon Tunisia team regularly organizes hackathons with new generation themes and helps to spread the hackathon culture throughout Tunisia.

WEEKLY NEWSLETTER

We publish our weekly bulletin for information purposes in order to bring you up-to-date hackathons and new articles.

HACKATHON ANNOUNCEMENTS

One of the biggest services of our community is to announce and inform you about hackathons around the world and mostly in Turkey.

CONTENT PRODUCTION

Hackathon Tunisia team prepares the most up-to-date content in the fields of technology, software, design and entrepreneurship, in addition to the articles in the field of hackathon.

HACKATHONS AROUND THE WORLD

We announce these hackathons, which are thought to be difficult to reach, and we plan to increase the number of Turkish participants.

COLLABORATIONS, COMPETITIONS

Hackathon Turkey team helps organize hackathons across Turkey by providing consultancy services to hackathons that different organizations want to organize.
